Intro

SIMPLE. BOX. was developed for the Yogscast Game Jam 2020. The theme for the jam was 'Surprise'. So I created a light 3D platformer puzzle game that had the player collect puzzle pieces, and at the end of each level, the puzzle pieces would be placed to unveil the surprise picture. If the player misses a piece then the final image will not be fully finished.
