Intro

Digital Wizards was a project I worked on as my final project of Game Development year 2 at my University, NUA. Digital Wizards is a top down 3D rogue like where the player has to collect spells in order to defeat enemies and traverse a dangerous environment. The project had seven people (including myself) and my primary role was enemy AI and level design.

Enemy AI

This code snippet is from the enemy controller CPP file. It essentially creates behaviour tree and blackboard components, both used in Unreal Engine 4 to control AI. After that the enemy controller then checks to see if it possessing a pawn. If false or 'nullptr', it simply returns. If the check is not 'nullptr', then it casts to the enemy it is possessing and checks to see if that enemy has a behaviour tree component. If that enemy does, then it initialises the blackboard and executes the behaviour tree.

This is the behaviour tree for our basic punching slime enemy. The punching slime enemy would patrol, as shown by the sequence to the right, until it the player entered its detection radius. If the player entered its detection radius, a 'target' would be set and the slime enemy would start to walk towards the player.

We finished the project with three types of enemies. The slow punching slime, that patrolled around a level; a bomb rushing skeleton that, when the player is detected, runs toward the player and explodes; and finally a stationary mimic that only gets triggered once the player enters its radius.

Level Design

One of my other main tasks for Digital Wizards was designing and building the second level. Although I haven't much experience in level design, I decided to create a plan, and that helped a lot when it came to building the actual level in the Unreal Engine 4 editor.

The goal for the second level was to show off the enemy AI. So with that in mind, I designed the second half of the level (the bottom image) to be an arena. When the player enters the arena, a gate slams shut and big punching slime enemys spawn and ambush the player.
