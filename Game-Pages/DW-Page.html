Intro

Digital Wizards was a project I worked on as my final project of Game Development year 2 at my University, NUA. Digital Wizards is a top down 3D rogue like where the player has to collect spells in order to defeat enemies and traverse a dangerous environment. The project had seven people (including myself) and my primary role was enemy AI and level design.
