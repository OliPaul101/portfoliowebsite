A Little About Me

Hi there! I'm Oli Paul, a game developer currently at Norwich University of the Arts doing a BSc in Games Development. This website contains and represents all of my work related to games development! Feel free to hover over the images listed for more information about each project and if you wish to contact me my details are at the bottom.

Projects

Digital Wizards

  • UE4

  • C++

My role for Digital Wizards was programming and designing the AI using C++ and Unreal Engine 4. In the end we had three fully functional AI. An exploding skeleton, a punching slime and a mimic chest!

Dog Walking Simulator

  • UE4

  • Blueprint

Dog walking simulator is a project I am currently developing for the Safe in our World charity. I wanted to make a game that can be relaxing and casual but, also a game that would allow me show what I have learned developing AI in Unreal Engine.

Llama Party

  • Unity

  • C#

Llama Party is my most ambitous project yet. For my final project for year 3 at University, I have decided to make a card game. But, for the AI in this game, I am using Unity's ML (Machine Learning) agents to train them.

Brewmaster

  • UE5

  • C++

Brewmaster was a game designed and prototyped in a week, that me and others listed are continuing to make into a first build and 'proof of concept'. My main roles for this project was AI designer and programmer.

Hardest Route

  • UE5

  • Blueprint

Hardest Route was a top down dungeon crawler game developed for the Global Games Jam 2023. As we were a small team, I had a hand in everything from AI and gameplay to audio and level design.
